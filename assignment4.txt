10.
   Decimal to Binary

   step1:divide decimal number by 2
   step2:divide the obtained quotient
         by 2, and note the remainder again.
         until get zero as the quotient.   
   step3:write last to first (reverse order)
   
   Binary to Decimal

 Write the binary number and count the power
 of 2 from right to left, starting from 0
 onwards. Now each binary number has the 
 corresponding power of 2 starting from 
 right to left. So the most significant bit
 will have the highest power of 2. The final
 answer will be converted into a decimal
 number that is base 10.
